# Prompt Patterns

## Scene still prompt

`[subject], [action], [environment], [framing], [lighting], [style], [continuity anchors], high detail, production-ready visual`

## Kling motion prompt

`Image-to-video animation prompt: Camera [move]. Subject [motion]. Environment [secondary motion]. Pacing [slow/medium/fast]. 5-second shot. Keep visual continuity and realism. Use clear cinematic motion language, not vague hype.`

## Narration style prompt

`Warm/confident/urgent/playful narration in [language], natural spoken rhythm, concise, emotionally aligned to the scene.`

## Music intent prompt

`Instrumental [genre] track for a [tone] short video, [BPM/energy], cinematic/modern/clean production, supportive under voiceover.`

## Example

Idea:
- luxury real-estate teaser

Scene still:
- Luxury modern penthouse at sunset, floor-to-ceiling windows, elegant interior, cinematic wide shot, warm golden light, premium architectural realism, high-end brochure aesthetic

Motion:
- Slow push-in through the living room toward the skyline, subtle curtain movement, warm sunset reflections, calm premium pacing

Narration:
- Step into a space where every detail feels elevated.
